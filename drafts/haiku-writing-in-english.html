Starting out

My relationship with poetry started when I went on a roadtrip across the southwest of the USA with my family. I was neither driver nor navigator, so I had time. Hours of dusty highways and rusty mesas, and similarly importantly, I had a travel notebook in my hands.

I'd bought that on a whim from a cafe bookstore near my workplace, a faux leather-bound, ecomaz notebook with two inserts. Maybe it was something about the thinness of each insert that made me itch to write, because it didn't feel too intimidating of a commitment? Either way, I started scribbling, here and there, just words that came to mind that amused me during the hours of driving from town to town.

Still, I couldn't write for five hours straight in a shaky car or I'd puke over the seats, so I ended up reading a couple of pre-downloaded books as well. Books, specifically, about poetry.
