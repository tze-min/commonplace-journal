Materials used

I wanted the cheap stuff to play around with first:

  • Pencils and ink: Pentel Energize 0.5 and Sakura Pigma Micron 03
  • Paints: Faber-Castell 12 watercolours
  • Paper: ecomaz travel journal

Tutorials referenced

I don't want to pressure myself; journaling gave my mind clarity. But that doesn't mean I don't want to improve my sketching skills, because drawing and painting engaged me with my surroundings, made me pay attention to them, and as a result, made me remember what I'd been doing and where I'd been.

These YouTubers' videos helped me make better - and more fun - sketches:

  • TobySketchLoose: Urban Sketching for Beginners - Step by Step - Tutorial
  • Finelinernerd: 5 Easy & Essential Watercolor Techniques for Beginners
  • 10,000hrs: The Books I Use to Self-Learn Art --- general to specific
